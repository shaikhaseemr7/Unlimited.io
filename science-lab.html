Science 11th & 12th Lab

Interactive resources and experimental guides tailored for 11th and 12th-grade students in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.

Physics

⚡ Ohm's Law and Resistance

Investigate the relationship between voltage, current, and resistance using circuit boards and multimeters.

🔭 Optics: Focal Length of Lenses

Determine the focal length of convex and concave lenses using an optical bench.

〰️ Simple Pendulum

Study the time period of a simple pendulum and calculate the acceleration due to gravity (g).

Chemistry

⚗️ Acid-Base Titration

Determine the concentration of an unknown acid or base solution using a standard solution and indicators.

🧂 Salt Analysis

Qualitative analysis to identify the cations and anions present in an inorganic salt mixture.

🌡️ Enthalpy of Dissolution

Measure the heat changes during the dissolution of salts like potassium nitrate or copper sulfate.

Biology

🔬 Onion Root Tip Mitosis

Prepare and observe slides of onion root tips to identify different stages of cell division (mitosis).

🌱 Plasmolysis in Epidermal Peels

Observe the effects of hypertonic solutions on plant cells using Rhoeo or Tradescantia leaves.

🧬 DNA Extraction

Extract and visualize crude DNA from plant materials like bananas or strawberries using simple household reagents.
